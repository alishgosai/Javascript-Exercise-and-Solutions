### **Hint**
![Hint](https://img.shields.io/badge/Hint:-blue)
Apply the formula for `Fahrenheit` to `Celsius` conversion.

### **Expected Output**
![Output](https://img.shields.io/badge/Output:-blue)
The function should return the temperature in `Celsius`.
